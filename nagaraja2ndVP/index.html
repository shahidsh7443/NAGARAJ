About Nagaraja Rao

Nagaraja Rao is a true Toastmaster at heart and carries the fervor of Toastmasters wherever he goes. He has applied the lessons he learnt in Toastmasters in his business and has achieved remarkable success. Nagaraja has coached several individuals to become champion speakers and great leaders. His ability to generate simple solutions to complex issues, along with his pleasant nature, makes him an excellent mentor.

His love for coffee and a penchant for entrepreneurship have blended perfectly to create business success. Nagaraja is the executive director of Ganesh Food Products (Pvt) Ltd., which owns the brand Bayar´s Coffee. His company also has exclusive manufacturing arrangements with major brands such as:

As an entrepreneur, Nagaraja is a part of the United Coffee Association of India; the Indian Coffee Trade Association, and the Federation of Karnataka Chambers of Commerce and Industry.

He enjoys playing sports, and is an active member of the Karnataka State Rifle Association, India.

He believes that an integral part of being a leader is giving back to society. He does community service and is part of Shivalli Smartha Brahmana Mahaparishath.

Nagaraja loves travelling around the world and makes it a point to visit one of the local Toastmasters club whenever possible. He understands that being a leader is a responsibility that involves making a difference. Thus his theme: BE THE DIFFERENCE, THAT MAKES A DIFFERENCE.

Why Nagaraja Rao

As District Governor of India and Sri Lanka, Nagaraja oversaw one of the most diverse districts in all of Toastmasters. He has faced and overcome a multitude of challenges that most District Governors never face. India is the third largest country in Asia and the seventh largest in the world, and has a population in excess of 1.2 billion people, with 22 official languages and more than 2000 dialects. His district was the epitome of diversity. The amount of travel and dedication needed to cover such a large area, for training and contests, would test any leader. Nagaraja not only succeeded in doing this, but excelled - ending his LGET and District Governor years as Number one in the world!

Nagaraja is defined by his passion for Servant Leadership. He firmly believes in the Toastmasters tagline Where Leaders Are Made. He has seen how Toastmasters has transformed him into a leader in his business and community. He is now keen to enable others to use this program to make a difference to themselves and others. He is eager to demonstrate his belief that we all can 'BE THE DIFFERENCE, THAT MAKES A DIFFERENCE'.

As an International Officer, he would bring his experience of 30 years in International business and 15 years in Toastmasters leadership to bear on issues such as strategizing, policymaking, financial oversight, planning and marketing. His cumulative knowledge from living and working in such a diverse area of the world would help him serve the organisation well as it expands in a dynamic market.
